Crystal lettuces are an innovative new type that looks like an open iceberg lettuce, with frilled crunchy and juicy leaves.
It can be grown year-round and produces compact heads that are heavy. It is especially suited for hydroponic production that SMARTKAS utilises, under high-intensity light and in factories, because it does not heat up, twist, elongate or crack easily.

Crystal type has a sweet and crunchy bite, so it can replace iceberg in dishes that call for single leaf lettuces. Crystal lettuces are also delicious combined with hot and acidic dishes, such as the ones in Asian cuisine.
